How it works

Three steps to trusted credentials

AI-powered extraction meets immutable blockchain storage.

01

Upload & Extract

PNG, JPG, PDF, or DOCX — our OCR engine reads the document and uses a scoring algorithm to extract name, course, issuer, date, and ID.

02

Hash & Record

A deterministic SHA-256 hash is generated from the extracted fields and appended as a new block on the local blockchain ledger.

03

Verify & Report

Re-uploaded certificates are hashed and compared. Fuzzy matching catches minor OCR differences. Download a signed PDF report.

04

QR Code Access

Every issued certificate gets a QR code linking to its verification URL — easy for institutions and employers to scan and confirm.
